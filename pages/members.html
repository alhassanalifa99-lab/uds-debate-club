Our Membership

200+ Strong and Growing

The UDS Debate Society draws members from every faculty across the university. Our members range from first-year students discovering debate for the first time to seasoned competitors representing Ghana at national tournaments.

We don't require prior experience — just the desire to learn, grow, and contribute.
