Why Join Us

What You'll Gain

🎤

Public Speaking Confidence

Weekly practice sessions in a supportive environment will transform your ability to speak clearly, confidently, and persuasively in any setting.

🧠

Critical Thinking Skills

Learn to dissect complex issues, evaluate evidence, and construct airtight arguments — skills that translate directly to academic and professional success.

🌍

Civic Awareness

Engage with real issues affecting Ghana and the world. Our civic education programmes deepen your understanding of democracy, governance, and your rights.

🤝

Community & Network

Join a community of like-minded students and build friendships and professional connections that last well beyond university.

🏆

Competition Opportunities

Represent UDS at inter-university, regional, and national debate competitions, with coaching and preparation from senior members.

📜

Certificates & Recognition

Active members receive certificates of participation, awards, and letters of recommendation that strengthen your CV and graduate school applications.

The UDS Debate and Civic Education Society — building articulate, informed, and civically engaged leaders at the University for Development Studies, Tamale.
