Looking Back

Past Highlights

A snapshot of what we've accomplished in recent years.

2024

National University Debate Championship

UDS Debate Society reached the quarter-finals, our best national result to date.

2024

Constitution Day Civic Forum

Hosted a public forum on Ghana's constitutional rights, attended by over 300 students and community members.

2023

High School Debate Mentorship Programme

Trained and mentored debate teams from five secondary schools in the Tamale Metro area.

2023

Inter-Faculty Championship

Faculty of Law emerged champions; Faculty of Public Health were runners-up in a closely contested final.

2022

Northern Regional Voters Education Drive

Reached over 500 first-time voters with civic education ahead of the 2024 general elections.

2022

UDS Open Debate Tournament

Hosted UDS's first open debate tournament, welcoming teams from five universities across Ghana.
