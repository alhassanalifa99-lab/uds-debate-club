Who We Are

Nurturing Ghana's Next Generation of Civic Leaders

The UDS Debate and Civic Education Society is a student-led organisation at the University for Development Studies, Tamale. We are committed to developing articulate, informed, and engaged citizens through structured debate, civic education, and community outreach.

Our members come from diverse faculties across UDS, united by a shared passion for public discourse and service to society.

Learn More About Us
🎤 Public Speaking & Debate

We train members in formal debate formats, rhetoric, and public speaking — skills that serve them for life.

🏛️ Civic Education

We educate communities on democracy, governance, and citizens' rights, from campus to the wider Northern Region.

🏆 Competitions

We represent UDS in inter-university debate competitions across Ghana and internationally.
